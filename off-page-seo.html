Off-Page SEO

Off-Page SEO refers to all optimization activities performed outside the website to improve search engine rankings, authority, and trust.

Why Off-Page SEO is Important

  • Builds website authority
  • Improves domain trust
  • Increases organic traffic
  • Boosts Google rankings

Off-Page SEO Checklist

1. Blog Submission

Example: medium.com, blogger.com

2. Article Submission

Publishing long-form articles on high-authority sites.

3. Guest Posting

Writing content for other websites with backlinks.

4. Social Bookmarking

Sharing pages on platforms like Reddit, Mix.

5. Forum Submission

Answering questions on forums with helpful links.

6. Image & Infographic Submission

Uploading optimized images with backlinks.

7. Video Submission

Publishing videos on YouTube with SEO descriptions.

8. PPT & PDF Submission

Uploading presentations and PDFs on document-sharing sites.

9. Directory Submission

Submitting business details to directories.

10. Google My Business (GMB)

Optimizing local business profile for local SEO.

11. Press Release (PR)

Publishing news about your business.
