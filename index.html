What is SEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of optimizing a website to improve its visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs) like Google and Bing. SEO helps websites gain organic traffic and reach the right audience.

Types of SEO

SEO is divided into three main types:

  • On-Page SEO
  • Off-Page SEO
  • Technical SEO

On-Page SEO

On-Page SEO focuses on optimizing elements within the website to improve rankings and user experience.

Key Techniques:

  • Title tag and meta description optimization
  • Header tags (H1–H6)
  • Keyword optimization
  • Image optimization (alt text)
  • Internal linking
  • SEO-friendly URLs
  • Schema markup

Off-Page SEO

Off-Page SEO includes activities performed outside the website to build authority and trust.

Key Techniques:

  • Backlink building
  • Guest posting
  • Blog and article submission
  • Social bookmarking
  • Forum posting
  • Google My Business (GMB)

Technical SEO

Technical SEO ensures that search engines can crawl, index, and rank a website effectively.

Key Techniques:

  • Website speed optimization
  • Mobile-friendly design
  • SSL / HTTPS
  • XML sitemap
  • Robots.txt
  • Canonical tags
  • Fixing broken links

SEO Techniques Based on Practices

  • White Hat SEO: Ethical practices that follow search engine guidelines
  • Grey Hat SEO: Combination of ethical and risky methods
  • Black Hat SEO: Unethical practices that may lead to penalties
