What is SEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of optimizing a website to improve its visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs). SEO helps websites attract organic traffic, increase rankings, and reach the right audience.

Types of SEO

  • On-Page SEO – Optimizing website content and HTML elements
  • Off-Page SEO – Building authority through backlinks and external signals
  • Technical SEO – Improving crawling, indexing, and site performance

On-Page SEO

On-page SEO focuses on optimizing elements within the website to improve rankings and user experience.

  • Title tags & meta descriptions
  • Header tags (H1–H6)
  • Keyword optimization
  • Image optimization (ALT text)
  • Internal linking
  • SEO-friendly URLs
  • Schema markup

Off-Page SEO

Off-page SEO includes activities performed outside the website to increase trust, authority, and rankings.

  • Backlink building
  • Guest posting
  • Blog & article submission
  • Social bookmarking
  • Forum participation
  • Google My Business (GMB)

Technical SEO

Technical SEO ensures that search engines can crawl, index, and understand a website efficiently.

  • Website speed optimization
  • Mobile-friendly design
  • SSL / HTTPS
  • XML sitemap
  • Robots.txt
  • Canonical tags
  • Fixing broken links

SEO Techniques

  • White Hat SEO: Ethical practices following search engine guidelines
  • Grey Hat SEO: Moderate-risk techniques
  • Black Hat SEO: Unethical practices that may cause penalties
