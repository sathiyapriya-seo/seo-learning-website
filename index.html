Keyword Research for SEO

What is Keyword Research?

Keyword research for SEO is the foundation of search engine optimization. It helps you understand what users are searching for in Google and how competitive those keywords are.

Why Keyword Research is Important

  • Helps target the right audience
  • Improves Google rankings
  • Increases organic traffic
  • Guides content creation

Free Keyword Research Tools

You can perform keyword research using the following free tools:

  • Google Search (Autocomplete & Related Searches)
  • Google Keyword Planner
  • Google Trends
  • Ubersuggest (Free version)

Step-by-Step Keyword Research Process

  1. Choose a main topic related to your website
  2. Find keyword ideas using Google Search
  3. Analyze search volume and competition
  4. Select primary and secondary keywords
  5. Use keywords naturally in content

Example of Keywords

Primary Keyword: keyword research for SEO

Secondary Keywords: free keyword research tools, SEO keyword analysis

What is On-Page SEO?

On-page SEO refers to optimizing the content and HTML elements of a webpage so that it ranks better in search engines.

Why On-Page SEO is Important

  • Helps Google understand your content
  • Improves keyword rankings
  • Increases organic traffic
  • Enhances user experience

Key On-Page SEO Factors

Title Tag

The title tag should include the primary keyword and be under 60 characters.

Meta Description

The meta description should summarize the page content and encourage clicks.

Headings (H1, H2, H3)

Use one H1 tag and organize content using H2 and H3 tags.

Content Optimization

Write clear, original content using keywords naturally.

Internal Linking

Link related pages such as Keyword Research and Technical SEO.

Simple On-Page SEO Checklist

  • One primary keyword per page
  • SEO-friendly URL
  • Optimized images with alt text
  • Internal links added
  • Mobile-friendly design
